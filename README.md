## Projects

### CFD & Aerodynamics Projects
- Ran 2D and 3D CFD simulations on airfoils and wings using [ANSYS Fluent].
- Post-processed results to visualize pressure fields, streamlines, and lift/drag characteristics.
- Compared simulation results to theory (thin airfoil theory, lifting line, etc.).

### Structural Analysis & FEA
- Built finite element models of cantilever beams and beam-like structures.
- Evaluated stress distributions, deflections, and safety factors under various loading conditions.
- Used [ANSYS Mechanical / Abaqus].

### SAB P-51D Project (Siemens NX) – Wing Team
- Modeled the wing geometry and internal structure in Siemens NX.
- Performed basic aerodynamic analysis and structural checks.
- Collaborated with a multi-person team to integrate wing design with fuselage and control surfaces.
